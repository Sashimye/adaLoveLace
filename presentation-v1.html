Manage projects.

The Problem

Project info is everywhere. Except where you need it.

Teams today juggle Slack, email, spreadsheets, and informal chats just to answer one question: "Where are we on this project?" By the time someone has the answer, half the meeting is over.

🗂️

Information is scattered

Tasks live in emails, Slack threads, and sticky notes. Nothing is in one place.

⏱️

Priorities are unclear

Developers waste time figuring out what to work on instead of actually shipping.

🔇

Blockers go unnoticed

Task dependencies are discovered too late, causing cascading delays.

📉

Progress is invisible

Managers can't answer "where are we?" without chasing multiple people first.

Who It's For

Built around real people and real pain.

👩‍💼
Project Manager
Konoka

Responsible for multiple client projects at an 8-person communication agency. She coordinates designers, writers, and developers — and needs a real-time view of everything without chasing people down.

  • Spends 30+ min per day just collecting status updates
  • Can't spot blockers until they've already caused delays
  • Struggles to redistribute tasks quickly when someone is out
👨‍💻
Junior Developer
The Dev

Junior developer at an 8-person startup, juggling several features in parallel. Task assignments arrive through Slack, email, and hallway conversations — with no clear priority or dependency tracking.

  • Loses time every morning figuring out what to work on next
  • Risks working on a low-priority task while a blocker sits idle
  • Discovers dependencies too late, creating downstream delays
V1 Features

Five features. One focused week.

We scoped V1 around the features that eliminate the biggest daily friction points — the ones that deliver real value immediately, without overbuilding.

🃏

Project Card

The entry point for every project. Create a project with a title, description, status, assigned members, and dates. The foundation everything else is built on.

Core — Built First
✅

To Do List

A personal task view for each team member. See all your tasks across every project in one place — no more hunting through multiple boards to know what's next.

Productivity
📅

Calendar

Visualize tasks and deadlines over time. Spot deadline clusters, plan your week, and see what's coming — without leaving the app.

Visibility
💬

Whispers

Private 1:1 messaging between members of the same organization. Quick, contextual exchanges without the noise of public channels or jumping to an external app.

Collaboration
🏆

Achievement

A live feed of recently completed tasks across the team. Progress made visible — recognition built in, momentum sustained, wins celebrated.

Motivation
Tech Stack
